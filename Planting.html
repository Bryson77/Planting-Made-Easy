Welcome to planting made easy!

Planting Made Easy is a website and mobile app created to help South Africans grow their own food gardens — easily, affordably, and confidently.

Food insecurity is a growing challenge across the world, and South Africa is no exception. Many communities lack access to fresh, healthy food — but we believe the solution can start right at home, in the soil.

Our goal is to use simple technology to empower individuals, families, and schools to grow sustainable gardens — whether in backyards, schoolyards, or small plots. We offer tools, tips, and guides tailored to South African conditions.

With the right knowledge and support, anyone can grow their own food. Let's grow together — one seed at a time.
