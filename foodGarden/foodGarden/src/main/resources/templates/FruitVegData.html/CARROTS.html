CARROT

Growth Duration: Full-sized carrots take 70–80 days; shorter types 55–65 days.

Optimal Growing Seasons: Plant in early spring or autumn; carrots tolerate light frost.

Yield Per Area: Commercial yields range from 9 to 18 tons per acre; spacing affects root quality.

Water Requirements: Needs 2.5–5 cm of water weekly; consistent moisture prevents cracking.

Soil Requirements: Loose, sandy loam with pH 6.0–6.8; free of stones and clumps.

Sunlight Needs: Requires 6–8 hours of direct sunlight daily.
