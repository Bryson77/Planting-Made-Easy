CUCUMBER

Growth Duration:

Field-grown cucumbers mature in 55 to 60 days, while greenhouse varieties may take over 70 days.



Optimal Growing Seasons:

Plant when soil temperatures exceed 15째C. Cucumbers are sensitive to frost and grow best in warm conditions.



Water Requirements:

Cucumbers are heavy feeders, requiring 1 to 2 inches of water per week. Increase watering during flowering and fruiting stages.



Soil Requirements:

Fertile, well-drained soil with a pH of 6.5 to 7.5 is ideal. Add compost or aged manure to boost fertility.



Sunlight Needs:

Full sun exposure is essential for healthy growth and fruit development.
