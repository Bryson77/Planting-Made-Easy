CABBAGE

Growth Duration:

Matures in 60–100 days after transplanting; 90–120 days from seed.



Optimal Growing Seasons:

Plant in early spring or late summer; prefers cooler months.



Yield Per Area:

Yields range from 25 to 70 tons per hectare; spacing affects head size.



Water Requirements:

Needs 25–40 mm of water weekly; consistent moisture is key.



Soil Requirements:

Fertile, loamy soil with pH 6.0–6.8; amend with compost or manure.



Sunlight Needs:

Requires 6–8 hours of full sun daily.



Fertilizer Needs:

Use balanced NPK fertilizer; organic options like compost are effective.
