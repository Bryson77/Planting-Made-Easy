AVOCADO

Growth Duration: Standard avocado trees take 4 to 15 years to bear fruit, while grafted trees can produce within 2 to 3 years.

Optimal Growing Seasons: Spring and summer promote active growth and flowering. In cooler months, growth slows or stops, especially in frost-prone areas.

Yield Per Area: Average yields range from 12 to 16 tons per hectare, with top-performing farms reaching up to 22 tons.

Water Requirements: Requires 1,000 to 1,300 mm of water annually. Regular watering is crucial during flowering and fruit set.

Soil Requirements: Prefers well-drained soil with a pH between 5 and 7. Rich organic matter improves root health and nutrient uptake.

Sunlight Needs: Needs 6 to 8 hours of direct sunlight daily. Protect young trees from strong winds and excessive heat.

Temperature Tolerance: Thrives between 16°C and 29°C. Temperatures outside this range can hinder growth or damage fruit.
