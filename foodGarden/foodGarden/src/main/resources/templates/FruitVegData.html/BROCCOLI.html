BROCCOLI

Growth Duration:

Broccoli takes 70 to 100 days to mature from seed, depending on the variety. Early types are ready in 60–75 days, while late-season varieties may take longer.



Optimal Growing Seasons:

Best planted in cooler months—February to April. Ideal temperatures range from 10°C to 20°C, which promote compact head formation.



Yield Per Area:

Yields range from 5 to 12 tons per hectare, averaging around 8 tons. Proper spacing, pest control, and soil fertility are key to maximizing output.



Water Requirements:

Requires 250–300 mm of water per week. Soil should remain evenly moist, especially during head development.



Soil Requirements:

Prefers rich, loamy, well-drained soil with pH between 6.0 and 7.0. Organic matter like compost or aged manure boosts nutrient availability.



Sunlight Needs:

Needs 6–8 hours of full sun daily. In hot climates, partial shade can help prevent premature bolting.
